The Best Movies According to Maira

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Kung Fu Panda

I like this movie because it teaches that being true to yourself is the key to greatness.

The Lion King

I like this movie because of its powerful storytelling, unforgettable music, and deep life lessons about overcoming fear and embracing one's true path.

The SpongeBob SquarePants

I like this movie because of its wacky humor, lovable characters, and a heartfelt message about believing in yourself, no matter how silly you may seem..
